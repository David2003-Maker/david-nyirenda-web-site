About Me

I am David Nyirenda, an Accountant with over 20 years of experience, a member of the Zambia Institute of Chartered Accountants. I have worked under the Ministry of Local Government of Zambia in various local authorities across different provinces. My career spans roles including Manager Finance at Lusaka City Council, Treasurer at Manyinga Town Council, and Finance Officer at Nakonde and Sioma Town Councils. My focus is on local governance, community engagement, and socioeconomic development.

Key Achievements:

  • Led a workers' union of over 3000 workers at Lusaka City Council.
  • Developed a robust accounting system for Lusaka's largest market.
  • Supervised the construction of administrative blocks and markets in Manyinga.
  • Procured multimillion earth-moving equipment for Nakonde's road maintenance.
  • Participated in elections from Presiding Officer to Finance Officer.
